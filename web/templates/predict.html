Quick glossary

  • You vs model — You pick XIs; the model estimates who wins and player ranks.
  • Monte Carlo — Many fake reruns of the match to get a stable win %; more = smoother, slower.
  • Registry / squad / shrinkage — See the boxed section in the form for what each toggle does.

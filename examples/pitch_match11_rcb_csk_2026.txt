Pitch report: The square boundaries are 68m and 61m; the straight boundary is at 74m. Great surface, one of the best in India for batting. There's a little bounce; that's why RCB loads its team with taller bowlers. The new ball is always the key, if you strike with it, there's a chance, but if you don't, that's where the big scores come from. There are a lot of cracks on the surface, it's not a ground where spin plays a huge role. Pacers predominantly bowl 65% of the overs. But there's a always a role to play for the spinners. If you look at Krunal Pandya and what he did last year - hitting the top of the stump length - if you're full as a spinner, it's one of the most difficult grounds to bowl on, reckon Ian Bishop and Faf du Plessis, in their pitch report
Venue: M Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru.
